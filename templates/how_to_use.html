How to Use Picture Recreator

Welcome to Picture Recreator! Here's how you can use our app to recreate your pictures:

  1. Navigate to the Home page.
  2. Click on the "Choose File" button to select the image you want to upload.
  3. Select the operation you want to perform from the dropdown menu:
    • Convert it into JPG
    • Convert it into PNG
    • Convert it into Grayscale
    • Convert it into WEBP
  4. Click on the "Submit" button.
  5. Your image will be processed, and you will see a link to the processed image. Click on the link to view or download your new image.

If you encounter any issues or have questions, feel free to visit the Contact Us page for support.
